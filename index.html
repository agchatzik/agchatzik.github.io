About

I am an electrical engineer with specialization in electrical energy and Information processing systems. Initially, I worked on projects related to High voltage engineering, however my passion for coding and Artificial Intelligence motivated me to pursue a Master’s Degree on ‘’Information Processing Systems and Machine Intelligence’’.

I was always enthusiast with computers. While my affinity for programming was present throughout my education, there was no focus; nothing in particular stood out to me. As I went into the masters programme, I came across the field of Computer Vision (CV) and I was fascinated. The premise of the field was full of interesting ideas and the possible applications seemed endless but, most importantly, there were a lot of interesting and challenging problems left to be solved.

All of these factors combined made me choose Computer Vision as the field I wanted to study on. As I read more about it, I became increasingly interested in Visual Odometry 3D reconstruction and Point Cloud proccessing that’s what I am focusing on to this day. My favourite applications of CV lie in autonomy engineering with UAV's and Self-Driving Cars and I enjoy utilizing both computational geometric and machine learning approaches to my solutions.

I am a driven Software Engineer continuously developing my skills on machine learning and computer vision which I use as technological tools in Robotics/Cybernetics applications, and AI.

My programming languages of choice include C++ and Python and I am very familiar with frameworks such as OpenCV, PCL, Tensorflow/Keras and PyTorch both in Windows and (but mostly in) Linux.

Point Cloud Rectification

Surface reconstruction from RGB-D images. Point clouds registration using the ICP algorithm, while achieving faster convergence using filtering techniques. Clustering of 3D mesh objects applying a triangulation method and a region growing algorithm.

  • Repository

Video Object surveillance

A particle filter based tracking algorithm for video appplication. The observation model that gives the likelihood is be based on the use of edges. Specifically, an edge detector runs on one prior-based window for positioning and extracting edges.

  • Repository

Vehicles detection

Detection of region of interest (ROI) and application of appropriate mask in image sequence, to detect vehicles moving in highway.

  • Repository

Video stabilization

Computation of Optical flow (Lukas- Kanade implemetation) and edge detection in video frames for the purpose of video stabilization.
